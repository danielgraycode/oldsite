About Me

Who I am and what I do




Hi there, my names Daniel. I'm a developer/programmer/whateveryouliketocallus and I make things. I spend most of my time making things or playing the odd game. In terms of the things I make, I love to work with languages like nodeJS, bit of python and all things webby. I started off by always finding it cool to make things, so I had a go myself. I launched open my web browser, and looked up how to make a website. Then I launched up notepad++, wrote a few lines in html and declared it my first site. (Get a picture of that here).

After that I was touch and go till about a year ago, thats when I really started. I worked on tutorials and looked at current sites, then made websites with that knowledge. I also started working with nodejs to make bots for discord at this time, starting with Dancord. This really got my juices flowing.

Since then I've just been making things in my spare time here and there, some websites, web apps (only I really use them..) and more discord bots. I'm still learning, believe it or not and hope to progress as time comes on. As for what I make the stuff in, I'm very hooked to Codeanywhere, an awesome cloud IDE that supports most languages on the planet. Thanks for reading!
